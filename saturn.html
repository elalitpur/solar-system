Saturn üíç

Saturn is a gas giant famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 120,500 km
  • Distance from Sun: 1.4 billion km
  • Day Length: 10.7 hours
  • Year Length: 29 Earth years
  • Type: Gas Giant
  • Moons: 146

Structure & Composition

Hydrogen and helium with a rocky/metal core.

Atmosphere

Yellow-brown clouds; winds up to 500 m/s.

Unique Features

  • Extensive ring system.
  • Hexagon-shaped storm at north pole.
  • Powerful magnetosphere.
