Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It's rocky and has lots of craters like the Moon.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Distance from Sun: 58 million km
  • Day Length: 59 Earth days
  • Year Length: 88 Earth days
  • Type: Rocky (Terrestrial)
  • Moons: 0

Structure & Composition

Mercury has a big iron core that makes up most of it, with a thin rocky outer layer.

Atmosphere

It has almost no atmosphere, just a thin layer of gases from the Sun and space rocks.
