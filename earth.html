Earth is our home, the only planet with life and liquid water.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 12,756 km
  • Distance from Sun: 150 million km
  • Day Length: 24 hours
  • Year Length: 365.25 days
  • Type: Rocky (Terrestrial)
  • Moons: 1 (The Moon)

Structure & Composition

Layers: Solid inner core (iron/nickel), liquid outer core, mantle (hot rock), crust (where we live).

Atmosphere

78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, protects from Sun and space rocks.

Unique Features

  • 71% covered in water.
  • Magnetic field creates auroras.
  • Tectonic plates cause earthquakes/volcanoes.
  • Seasons from axis tilt.
