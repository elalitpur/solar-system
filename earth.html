Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It formed about 4.54 billion years ago and is located about 149.6 million kilometers from the Sun.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Age: 4.54 billion years
  • Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km
  • Day Length: 24 hours
  • Year Length: 365.25 days
  • Surface: 71% water, 29% land

Structure of Earth

Earth has four main layers:

  • Crust: The thin outer layer where we live.
  • Mantle: A thick layer of hot, semi-solid rock.
  • Outer Core: Liquid iron and nickel.
  • Inner Core: Solid iron and nickel.

Atmosphere

Earth's atmosphere protects life and helps regulate temperature. It is made up of:

  • 78% Nitrogen
  • 21% Oxygen
  • 1% Other gases (Argon, Carbon Dioxide, etc.)

Why Earth is Special

  • Only known planet with liquid water on the surface
  • Has a breathable oxygen-rich atmosphere
  • Protected by a magnetic field
  • Home to millions of species, including humans

Movement

Earth rotates on its axis every 24 hours, causing day and night. It revolves around the Sun once every 365.25 days, causing seasons.
