Neptune is the farthest planet, an ice giant with strong winds.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 49,528 km
  • Distance from Sun: 4.5 billion km
  • Day Length: 16 hours
  • Year Length: 165 Earth years
  • Type: Ice Giant
  • Moons: 16

Structure & Composition

Icy materials (water, methane, ammonia) over rocky core; no solid surface.

Atmosphere

Hydrogen, helium, methane (blue color); winds up to 2,000 km/h.

Unique Features

  • Great Dark Spot storm.
  • 5 rings and arcs.
  • Triton moon has geysers.
