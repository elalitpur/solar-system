Jupiter is the biggest planet, a gas giant with colorful clouds and big storms.

Basic Facts

  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Distance from Sun: 778 million km
  • Day Length: 9.9 hours
  • Year Length: 12 Earth years
  • Type: Gas Giant
  • Moons: 95+

Structure & Composition

Mostly hydrogen and helium; liquid hydrogen ocean, possible rocky core.

Atmosphere

Clouds of ammonia and water; winds up to 539 km/h.

Unique Features

  • Great Red Spot: Giant storm bigger than Earth.
  • Strong magnetic field creates auroras.
  • Faint rings of dust.
